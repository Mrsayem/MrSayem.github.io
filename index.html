Research Work

Focus: deep learning for medical image segmentation — clinically impactful, lightweight, production-ready architectures.

Manuscript in Preparation · 2026
Hybrid ResNet-50 Architecture for Multi-Dataset Breast Ultrasound Lesion Segmentation
Arafat Hossain Sayem, Tania Afroz Toma
Target: IEEE Journal of Biomedical and Health Informatics (JBHI)

Proposes a ResNet-50 encoder enhanced with ASPP multi-scale bridge, a malignant-aware Balanced CBAM attention mechanism, and anisotropic row-column decoder convolutions. Trained on a combined BUSI + BUS-UCLM cohort (1,133 images), achieving strong cross-dataset generalization to unseen UDIAT data — demonstrating true clinical transferability.

Work in Progress · 2026
MVSegNet: Lightweight MobileNetV3-Based Fetal Brain Ventricle Segmentation with Joint Severity Classification
Arafat Hossain Sayem
Target: Medical Image Analysis / MICCAI

A lightweight MobileNetV3-backboned segmentation network featuring multi-scale attention modules (MSAM) and adaptive boundary refinement blocks (ABRB). Designed for improved ventricle boundary delineation, small-lesion sensitivity, and fast low-resource clinical inference — jointly classifying ventriculomegaly severity alongside segmentation.

Backbone MobileNetV3
Task Segmentation + Classification
Target Low-resource inference
