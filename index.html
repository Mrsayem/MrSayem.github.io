Research Work

Focus: clinically impactful deep learning architectures for medical image analysis.

Manuscript in Preparation · 2026 IEEE JBHI
Hybrid ResNet-50 Architecture for Multi-Dataset Breast Ultrasound Lesion Segmentation
Arafat Hossain Sayem, Tania Afroz Toma
IEEE Journal of Biomedical and Health Informatics (JBHI)

Proposes a ResNet-50 encoder with ASPP multi-scale bridge, a malignant-aware Balanced CBAM attention module, and anisotropic row-column decoder convolutions. Trained jointly on BUSI and BUS-UCLM (1,133 images), with strong cross-dataset generalisation to unseen UDIAT data — a critical marker of clinical deployability.

Work in Progress · 2026 Target: MICCAI / MedIA
MVSegNet: Lightweight MobileNetV3-Based Fetal Brain Ventricle Segmentation with Joint Severity Classification
Arafat Hossain Sayem
Target: Medical Image Analysis / MICCAI

A lightweight MobileNetV3-backboned segmentation network integrating multi-scale attention modules (MSAM) and adaptive boundary refinement blocks (ABRB). Targets improved ventricle boundary delineation, small-lesion sensitivity, and fast low-resource clinical inference — with a joint head for ventriculomegaly severity classification.

Backbone MobileNetV3 Task Seg + Classification Target Low-resource inference Dataset Fetal brain MRI (Zenodo)
