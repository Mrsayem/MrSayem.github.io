Submitted IEEE JBHI 2026 Breast ultrasound

Hybrid ResNet-50 Architecture for Multi-Dataset Breast Ultrasound Lesion Segmentation

Arafat Hossain Sayem, Tania Afroz Toma
Submitted to IEEE Journal of Biomedical and Health Informatics

Develops a generalizable breast ultrasound lesion segmentation model for diverse imaging settings. The architecture combines a ResNet-50 encoder, ASPP multi-scale bridge, malignant-aware Balanced CBAM attention, and anisotropic row-column decoder convolutions.

GitHub Repository
Training dataBUSI + BUS-UCLM, 1,133 images
GeneralizationEvaluated on unseen UDIAT
Core methodResNet-50 + ASPP + CBAM
